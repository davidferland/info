About me

The evolution of the information technologies in the past years have brought us in a fast pace reality where becoming obsolete can come sooner than later for those who do not update their skills constantly. Conscious of this reality, I continuously update my technical and managerial skills. I also insist on my teammates and subordinates that they keep on doing the same for the benefit of their employer and the success of their own professional career and I exercise permanent coaching for them to succeed. I set high goals for myself, my co-workers and ensure a continuous monitoring to achieve them.
